  • Tesla Model S

The Model S Plaid has a range of up to 390 miles (628 km) on a single charge, making it one of the longest-range electric cars on the market. It can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (97 km/h) in less than 2 seconds, making it one of the quickest production cars ever made. The car also features a redesigned interior, including a new steering wheel design and a landscape-oriented touchscreen display. It also includes several advanced driver-assistance features, including automatic lane changing and summoning the car to come to the driver's location. The Model S Plaid is positioned as a high-performance luxury car, with a starting price of around $130,000. It competes with other high-end luxury cars such as the Porsche Taycan and the Audi e-tron GT.
